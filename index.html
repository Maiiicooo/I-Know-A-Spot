About I know a spot

Discover and share the best hidden chill spots around you.

Why this website?

There aren't many places online where you can find truly good spots to relax – think peaceful parks, hidden beaches, or cozy hideaways. I built this platform so that everyone can discover and share their favorite spots with others.

How does it work?

  • Search: Use the search bar or explore the map to find spots near you.
  • View: Click on a spot to see details and photos.
  • Add: Know a great place? Click "➕ Add Spot" and share it easily.
  • Approval: Every spot is manually reviewed before appearing on the map.
